About Me

My name is Kojo Otchere and I am an enthusiastic and result-driven Systems Engineer with a rich experience in various industry sectors including logistics & supply chain, enterprise support & development, and inside sales & business development. My technical expertise, problem-solving acumen, and strong work ethic make me a valuable asset to any team. Currently, I serve as a Systems Engineer at General Dynamics Information Technology, where I am responsible for providing comprehensive engineering support to various subcontract, sourcing, procurement, and supply chain activities. Here, I have had the opportunity to act as a crucial technical contact and a liaison, creating strategic relationships with suppliers and establishing performance metrics to streamline processes. Before this, I was with MicroStrategy, Inc as a Support Engineer. During my tenure, I provided strategic engineering guidance for the implementation and rollout of new software, optimized SQL scripts, built strong relationships with high-priority clients, and monitored health, performance, and operational trends to proactively identify and solve issues. Earlier in my career, I was a System Analyst at Akira Technologies, Inc., where I honed my skills in proposal development, solution implementation, and team coordination.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Systems Engineering from The George Washington University. To further enhance my skills, I have earned a variety of certifications including the AWS Technical Professional and MicroStrategy Analyst Master Bootcamp.
